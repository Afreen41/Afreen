Overview

This luxury city hotel is the flagship hotel of the brand and is located next to the 18-hole golf course managed by Karnataka Golf Association. Ideally located in the Central Business District area with shopping avenues like Indiranagar, World-class Specialty Manipal Hospital and defense/ air force bases within the vicinity.

Tucked away from the city traffic lanes, the hotel offers 195 premium guest rooms categorized from Club rooms to 2 bedroom suites offering views of the golf course and the large, sprawling lawns within the premises. On can dine at the award-winning dining outlets serving global and local cuisines, well equipped gymnasium, roof-top 'infinity' temperature controlled swimming pool, meeting spaces for upto 1000 guests.
